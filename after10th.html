After completing 10th grade, students have multiple options to continue education or choose vocational paths:

Science Stream

Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Maths. Prepares for engineering, medical, and research careers.

Commerce Stream

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics. Prepares for B.Com, CA, banking, and business.

Arts Stream

History, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology. Leads to teaching, humanities, and civil services.

Vocational Courses

ITI, Diploma in Engineering, Animation, Fashion Designing, Hospitality, Agriculture, Retail Management.
